Twitter

Important Hackathon related announcements, such as submission information, and food distribution, will be released on our Twitter account. Make sure to follow us for up-to-the-minute announcements! Follow @IEEEHackathonUI

Deadline

The hackathon ends at Sat, Sept. 7, 2013 at 07:00:00 PM CDT. All projects must be registered before the end of submissions. Names will be called in random order, and if you fail to show up when your name is called, you will be dropped from the list of presenters.
